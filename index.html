What we do

    • Residential services

      Are you ready to transform your home into a technologically advanced oasis? Look no further than TruTech's Residential Services! We specialize in providing cutting-edge solutions to meet all your residential technology needs, ensuring your home is smarter, safer, and more connected than ever before. Experience blazing-fast internet with our top-of-the-line STARLINK and FIBER OPTIC options, delivering lightning-speed connectivity for all your online activities. Say goodbye to Wi-Fi dead zones with our advanced WIFI/MESH systems, providing seamless coverage throughout your entire home. Your safety is our priority, which is why we offer a comprehensive range of security solutions including CCTV, cameras, and security alarms to protect your loved ones and property around the clock. Take control of your home with our automation services, integrating sensors and managed services for ultimate peace of mind. Entertainment reaches new heights with our audio/video, TV, and satellite offerings, while our projectors create immersive cinematic experiences in the comfort of your own home. Stay connected with VOIP telephone systems and expert IT consultations, ensuring your home office is always up and running smoothly. From computers and software to printers and structured cabling, TruTech's Residential Services has everything you need to elevate your home technology experience.
